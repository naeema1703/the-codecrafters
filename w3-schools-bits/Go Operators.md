## Go Operators

Go Operators are symbols used to carry out operations on variables and values. They help in calculations, comparisons, logical evaluations, and more.

### Types of Go Operators

* Arithmetic Operators – Used for mathematical calculations: + (addition), - (subtraction), * (multiplication), / (division), % (modulus)

* Assignment Operators – Used to assign or update values in variables: =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=

* Comparison Operators – Used to compare values and return a boolean result (true or false): ==, !=, <, >, <=, >=

* Logical Operators – Used to combine or modify boolean expressions: && (AND), || (OR), ! (NOT)

* Bitwise Operators – Used to perform operations on individual bits of integers: & (AND), | (OR), ^ (XOR), << (left shift), >> (right shift)
